PROJECT TITLE: Hobnob- A bridge using Neural Networks

Developed a project that narrows the interaction gap between the deaf-dumb and normal people through recognizing hand signals in Indian sign language from real-time video and converting them to text and speech.

  • Implemented a user-friendly interface for seamless interaction between deaf-dumb and normal individuals.
  • Conducted data collection, pre-processing, and augmentation to enhance model robustness.
  • Collaborated with team members to enhance feature development and troubleshoot issues.
